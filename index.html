Terms of Service (TOS)

• Eligibility:
You must own or be creating a YouTube channel. We provide free logos/banners, but you’re responsible for how you use them.
• Rule Compliance:
You must follow all clan rules: required tags, weekly community posts, and the channel description update. Break them → removal.
• Content Requirements:
No misleading, harmful, scammy, or drama-bait content. Anything that damages the clan’s image = instant removal.
• Branding Support:
You can use IYZ graphics while you’re in the clan. If you leave, remove all IYZ branding immediately.
• Promotion Duties:
You must complete weekly IYZ-related promo tasks. Ignoring tasks repeatedly = removal.
• Behavior Expectations:
No misusing the clan name, no fake promotions, no causing problems. Stay active and respond to important announcements.
• Leaving or Removal:
Leave anytime, but remove all logos, links, and IYZ branding. Breaking rules = no warning, immediate removal.
• TOS Updates:
We can update these terms anytime. Staying in the clan means you accept the latest version.

About Us

Indian Youtubers Zone (IYZ) started with a simple idea: create a space where creators can actually grow, instead of the usual unorganized groups that go nowhere. That idea eventually became a structured community with clear rules, support, and real opportunities for creators who want to stay consistent.

IYZ was founded by Vaibhav Sundrani, who manages the community and leads the overall direction. He runs multiple YouTube channels, including the official IYZ channel and also operates a small digital e-store. Along with that, he manages several Discord servers, with two of them directly connected to the IYZ ecosystem.

IYZ continues to grow and improve as more creators join in.

By Vaibhav Sundrani
