How to Join

Joining IYZ is simple, but we only accept creators who are dedicated to growth and discipline.

The Process

Step 1: Application
Apply in our Discord Server. Follow the instructions and discuss your channel with the team professionally and without arguing.
Step 2: Channel Review
Our team reviews your content consistency and behavior. We don't care about subscriber counts, only your effort.
Step 3: Verification
If selected, your request will be accepted in the official creators server! Enjoy all the community resources there!
