Units & Roles

IYZ is organized into specialized units to ensure every creator and contributor has a clear role and support system.

Common Questions

Can we be part of all IYZ Units?
Yes, you can apply to become part of all IYZ Units!
Is it compulsory to be IYZ Provider if you are IYZ Creator?
No, it’s totally okay to be a IYZ Creator without being IYZ Provider but still, if you want more advantages, feel free to apply!
Do IYZ Workers have to do everything?
The work load depends on which work you choose [Video Editing, Graphic Designing, Web Developing and/or Networking] but IYZ Workers mostly get less work.
