Rules

To keep IYZ active, fair, and useful for serious creators, everyone follows a few basic rules.

IYZ Rules

What are the posting requirements?
Members must use #IYZ, #IndianYouTubersZone and #IYZCreators hashtags on shorts and follow weekly instructions.
Are there any branding rules?
Members must add required description lines and keep the official linktree in their channel link section.
What happens if rules are ignored?
Repeated rule-breaking leads to removal to protect the active members of the community.
