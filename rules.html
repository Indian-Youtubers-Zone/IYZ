Rules

To maintain a high-quality environment for all creators, we follow a strict set of discipline and branding rules.

IYZ Rules

What are the posting requirements?
Members must use #IYZ, #IndianYouTubersZone, and #IYZCreators hashtags on shorts and follow weekly instructions provided in the Discord server.
Are there any branding rules?
Members must add required description lines to their videos and keep the official IYZ Linktree in their channel's "Links" section.
What happens if rules are ignored?
Consistency and discipline are our core values. Repeated rule-breaking or toxic behavior leads to immediate removal to protect our active members.
